Final Year Project

BEng (Hons) Mechatronic Engineering · Asia Pacific University · 2026

Digital Twin of an Oil Palm Spraying AGV

NVIDIA Isaac Sim · Gaussian Splatting · YOLOv8-seg · ROS 2

Built a digital twin of an oil palm spraying AGV in NVIDIA Isaac Sim, enabling virtual pre-deployment testing without physical plantation visits. The system integrates a Gaussian Splatting-reconstructed plantation environment, YOLOv8-seg tree detection, a precision sprayer controller and real-time physical-virtual synchronisation. Five structured tests confirmed 90.5% sync accuracy, 96.3% AI detection rate and 96.0% spray actuation accuracy.
